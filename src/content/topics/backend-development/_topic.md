Backend development is the discipline of turning product rules into reliable services. A backend accepts requests, validates input, coordinates data storage, protects access, and returns predictable responses under load. This topic builds a practical junior-to-mid level foundation: how servers receive work, how REST APIs are shaped, how databases and indexes behave, how authentication differs from authorization, and how production systems stay observable and deployable.
