This roadmap organises your learning journey from writing your first HTML page to architecting production-grade frontend systems. Each phase describes the career level you are working toward, lists the exact lessons from other LevelUp topics to study, and gives you a concrete checklist of things to build or demonstrate before moving on. Use it as your north star: open a phase lesson to orient yourself, follow the cross-references into the technical topics for the actual deep-dives, then return here to confirm you have hit the milestones before advancing. The path is linear by design — every phase builds on the one before — but you can also jump straight to the phase that matches where you are right now and use the checklist to find your gaps.
