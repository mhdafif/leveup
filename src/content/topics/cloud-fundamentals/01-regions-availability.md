A region is a geographic area where a cloud provider runs infrastructure. Availability zones are isolated locations inside a region.

Choosing a region affects latency, cost, legal requirements, and disaster recovery options.

> [!TIP]
> Start single-region unless you have clear availability or latency requirements. Multi-region adds operational complexity.
